HISTORY


Beauboss Perfume was founded by Malaysian entrepreneur Athira Mohamed Shahid, inspired by her passion for fragrances and her experience discovering international scents during her career as a flight attendant. Seeing the limited access to luxurious yet affordable perfumes in the local market, she set out to create a brand that delivers high-quality, long-lasting scents at accessible prices. Beauboss began as a small pre-order business built on trust and customer satisfaction, and gradually grew into a well-recognised local perfume brand. Today, Beauboss continues to expand its range of signature fragrances while staying committed to quality, confidence, and everyday elegance



MISSION


To create high-quality, long-lasting fragrances that are affordable, accessible, and designed to inspire confidence and everyday elegance.


VISION


To become a leading perfume brand that offers luxurious, long-lasting fragrances at affordable prices, inspiring confidence and elegance in everyday life.
