Frequently Ask Question

  • How should I apply Beauboss perfume for the best results?

    • Apply perfume to pulse points where your body heat is highest: wrists, neck, behind the ears, and inside the elbows. Avoid rubbing your wrists together, as this can alter the scent. Spritz from a distance to ensure even distribution.


  • How long does Beauboss perfume last?

    • Beauboss perfume can last up to 12 hours on the skin, and if you spray it on your clothing, the scent can last up to 1 day.


  • How should I store my perfume?

    • It is best to store your perfume in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat. A dark cabinet or drawer is ideal. Avoid keeping it in the bathroom, as humidity can affect the fragrance.


  • Can I use Beauboss perfume on my clothes?

    • Yes, you can use perfume on your clothes, but be cautious as some perfumes may cause stains or discoloration. Test a small, inconspicuous area first. However, perfume on skin can last longer than on clothes.


  • Is it safe to use Beauboss perfume during pregnancy?

    • Beauboss is considered safe to use during pregnancy. However, some women may become more sensitive to scents during pregnancy, so opt for lighter, less overpowering fragrances.


  • Can I use Beauboss perfume during my prayer?

    • Yes, our perfume is wudhu friendly.
